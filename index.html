Exciting Projects

When it comes to games of diffrerent fracnhies we here at Troglodyte Studios are a fan of all different types of games which you can see in our selective projects.

Project Outgrowth

OutGrowth is an shooter based off a post apocalyptic New York City where you survive in one of the nuclear shelters spreaded out throughout the city and you must find a way to survive. Throughout your journey you will be faced with new threats amogst never seen in the city before as individuals who are as well attempting to survive in this nuclear city.

Project Rukia

Rukia takes place in alternative histroy where in the cold war the United States nuked russia and thus the world was led to damnation. However in russia some survived via the metro system they had placed in case of the endevor. However needing resources to survive you are forced to go onto the surface of the land finding the neccessary resources it takes to survive.

Project Endure

Endure is a Escape From Tarkov like first person shooter set in a dystopian cyberpunk future were humanity is being taken over by the cities AI called TITIAN which has locked down all exits to the city. Attempting to escape you must find gear to make sure you can survive against the TITIAN'S created robots and the other individuals attempting to escape by all means nesecarry.
